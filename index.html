About

Hi! I’m a computer science student, currently completing a Master’s degree at the University of Verona.

When I discovered programming I immediately fell in love with it: I gained the power to make the machine do exactly what I wanted. It shortly became my main passion, and still is.

I consider myself an hacker, in the positive sense: I like thinkering around with stuff, no matter if software or hardware, understanding how things work, and trying to improve them.

But don’t worry! I’m not the boring nerd that I may appear: I also like hanging out with friends, doing sports, music, litterature, and much more.
